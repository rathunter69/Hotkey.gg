This policy explains what information hotkey.gg ("hotkey.gg", "we", "us" — operated by [LEGAL ENTITY / OPERATOR]) collects when you use the trainer at [hotkey.gg domain], and what we do with it. Questions: hello@hotkey.gg.

What we collect

  • Account information. If you create an account, we store your email address and the display handle you choose. Much of the trainer can be used anonymously, without an account.
  • Gameplay data. Your drill times, scores, session results, and personal bests, so we can track your progress and rank you on public leaderboards (shown under your handle, not your email).
  • Browser local storage. We keep your theme choice, onboarding status, and personal-best times in your browser's local storage, and your sign-in session token is stored locally by our authentication provider. These are not advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.
  • Technical data. Our infrastructure providers automatically log standard technical information (such as IP address and browser type) to keep the Service secure and reliable. Web fonts are served by Google Fonts, which may receive your IP address as part of delivering them.

How we use it

We use this information to run the trainer, show your progress and leaderboards, secure accounts, respond to you, and improve the product. We do not sell your personal data, and the trainer does not display ads.

Service providers

Retention & deletion
