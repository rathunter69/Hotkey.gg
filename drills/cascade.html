Run the full cascade

Each tranche pays from the cash left — MIN against the balance, cascade down.
Three tranches (debt layers ranked by who’s paid first), four years, cash flowing strictly by seniority: the revolver is paid first, the term loan takes what’s left, and mezzanine — the most junior layer — gets the remainder — some years nothing, and the MIN has to say so. Every tranche rolls its balance across the years, and total debt re-ties each year.
