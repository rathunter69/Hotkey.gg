Fill down, fill right

Ctrl+D fills down, Ctrl+R fills right — one seed cell builds the whole table.
An empty quarterly buildout and a feed row from the data team. Three moves fill the page: PULL the feed (reference it once, fill across), FOOT it (write one SUM, fill it across), then the real trick — one mixed-anchor formula and a 2D fill: right THEN down, and twelve %-of-revenue cells build themselves. This is how a table gets filled without a table’s worth of typing.
