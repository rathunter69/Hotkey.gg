Build the discount rate

WACC = weights × costs — build the pieces, one line ties them.
Build the discount rate — WACC, the blended cost of debt and equity capital — with no shortcuts: unlever the peer beta at the peer’s structure, relever it at the target’s own debt-to-equity, compute cost of equity with CAPM, tax-effect the debt side, then weight both sides off one denominator. Five formulas, each feeding the next — the grader follows the chain, so a retyped number breaks it.
