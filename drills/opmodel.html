Build the operating model

An operating model is drivers → margins — grow revenue off one rate, cost it as % of sales, and the EBITDA line builds itself.
Five years of operating model from the drivers up. Revenue compounds off a single growth rate; COGS and opex are each a percent of that revenue; gross profit and EBITDA fall out, and the margin line reads the story. Every rate is anchored so the fill can’t drag it — build column one, fill the whole model right.
