Run the covenant table

Covenants are ratios with teeth — the table shows headroom before the breach.
Leverage is tested every quarter and the covenant STEPS DOWN — the question is never "are we compliant today", it's "which quarter pinches". Covenants test NET leverage: debt less cash, over LTM EBITDA. Compute it, headroom against the stepping max, a 1/0 compliance flag with a real IF, and pull the tightest quarter with MIN. When this table goes red, everyone you've ever met gets on a call.
