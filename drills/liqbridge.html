Bridge the liquidity — three cases

Liquidity = cash + revolver − needs — three cases, one bridge.
The first page of every RX deck: where does liquidity land, and in which case does it break? Cash plus undrawn revolver is the starting point; the bridge walks burn, asset sales and fees to an ending number; the cushion against minimum liquidity is the answer. Build it once, fill it across Base / Downside / Severe — the severe case is SUPPOSED to look ugly. That’s why the page exists.

Step by step

  4. cushion = ending − minimum · B14: =B12-B13 ↵ · fill — read which cases breach
  5. dress the answer: select B12:D12, ctrl+b
