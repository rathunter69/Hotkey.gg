Clean the export

Exports come dirty — delete junk rows (Ctrl+−), sort (Alt A S), refoot; the tape is yours.
The deal tape came out of the system DIRTY: a duplicated header row, a "--- PAGE 2 ---" break, and a stale SUBTOTAL row that will double-count the moment anyone foots the column. Scrub it — delete all three junk rows, sort what is real largest-first, and refoot the clean tape. Every export you will ever receive needs this pass.
train this drill → par 19 keystrokes · keyboard only · free
The keys
delete the 3 junk rows (alt h d r each) · select A4:B10 · alt a s d · foot at B11 → alt+= ↵ · ctrl+b
Step by step
  1. hunt the junk top to bottom: the duplicate header, the page break, the SUBTOTAL trap — on each row · alt h d r
  2. the tape is contiguous now: select A4:B10 (cursor in the size column) · alt a s d — largest first
  3. refoot it: B11 · alt+= ↵ · ctrl+b — a SUBTOTAL row would have poisoned this SUM
