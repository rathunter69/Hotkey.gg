The shortcuts in this drill
Alt walks: tap Alt, release, then the letters in sequence — each key steps one ribbon menu. On a Mac, tap ⌥ and use the same letters (KeyTips, Excel 2024+).
Alt h f c⌥ h f c on macFont color
Font color: blue inputs, black formulas — the oldest convention in banking models, still enforced.
Alt+=⌥ = on macAutoSum the adjacent range
AutoSum reads the block above or beside the cursor and proposes the SUM — foot rows and columns without typing a formula.
Ctrl+B⌘B on macBold
Bold marks structure: titles, total rows, anything the reviewer’s eye should land on first.
Alt h b p⌥ h b p on macTop border
Alt h k⌥ h k on macApply comma style
Comma style on any block of raw numbers — readable thousands in three keys.
Alt h 9⌥ h 9 on macRemove a decimal place
Strip decimals — share counts and headcounts don’t want cents.
Alt h a n⌥ h a n on macAlign middle (vertical)
Middle-align vertically when rows get tall — wrapped headers stay centered.
Alt h o i⌥ h o i on macAutofit column width
The ##### fix: autofit the column to its widest entry in three keys.
