Fold the detail away

Group rows (Shift+Alt+→), never hide them — the ⊞ tells the reader the detail’s still there.
Produce the summary view: quarterly totals with the monthly detail folded away. House rule — never hide rows; group them (Shift+Alt+→), then fold the detail (Alt A H) so a ⊞ shows the next reader where it lives. Three months under each quarter: group each block, fold each block, and the totals keep computing.

The shortcuts in this drill
Alt walks: tap Alt, release, then the letters in sequence — each key steps one ribbon menu. On a Mac, tap ⌥ and use the same letters (KeyTips, Excel 2024+).

Alt a h⌥ a h on macHide (fold) group detail
Collapse grouped detail to read the summary — the fold hides rows without deleting them.
