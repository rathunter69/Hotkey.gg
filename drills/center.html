Set the alignment

Alt H A R right-aligns numbers, A C centers headers — long titles center ACROSS (Ctrl+1), never merged.
Fix the alignment on the pipeline summary. House rule: headers centered over their columns, labels left-aligned, numbers and their totals right-aligned — and the total row is bold. The layout changes every run.
train this drill → par 18s · optimal 15 keys · keyboard only · free
The shortcuts in this drill
Alt walks: tap Alt, release, then the letters in sequence — each key steps one ribbon menu. On a Mac, tap ⌥ and use the same letters (KeyTips, Excel 2024+).
Alt h a c⌥ h a c on macAlign center
Center headers over their columns — alignment is half of readability.
Alt h a l⌥ h a l on macAlign left
Left-align labels — text reads from the left, numbers from the right.
Alt h a r⌥ h a r on macAlign right
Right-align headers over number columns so they sit flush with their digits.
Ctrl+B⌘B on macBold
Bold marks structure: titles, total rows, anything the reviewer’s eye should land on first.
Ctrl+1⌘1 on macOpen the Format Cells dialog
The master formatting dialog — number formats, alignment, borders in one place when a ribbon walk would take longer.

quarter headers B3:D3 → alt h a c · deal names A4:A6 → alt h a l · total row A7:D7 → alt h a r + ctrl+b · title A1:D1 → ctrl+1 a — centered ACROSS, never merged
