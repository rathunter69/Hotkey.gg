free ◆ always

Any club or cohort can start a desk for free — private boards, the roster hall, weekly quests, the guild board, school colors. This is the community layer, and it stays free.

Unlock every PRO feature for your analysts — full analytics history, ghost-cursor replays, unlimited follow-along, share-card themes. One invoice for the whole desk, no analyst expense reports. Buy the seats you need; add more anytime as the desk grows. Volume tiers for classes of 20+.
